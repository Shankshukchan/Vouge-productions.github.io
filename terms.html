Vogue Productions Model Engagement Agreement

1. Engagement & Scope of Work

  1. Upon registration, the Second Party shall be considered for modeling assignments with the First Party, subject to availability, suitability, and discretion.
  2. The First Party may provide optional workshops, training sessions, and career guidance to support the professional development of the Second Party.
  3. The Second Party shall receive exposure to industry professionals, photographers, and brands through the First Party’s promotional activities.

2. Commitment & Obligations

  1. Once a shoot date is confirmed, the Second Party shall be obligated to fulfill the assignment as agreed.
  2. Any unexcused absence or cancellation without 48-hour notice and valid reason shall result in a monetary penalty, recoverable from future payments.
  3. The First Party shall provide detailed shoot briefs and styling guidelines before the assignment.

3. Professional Conduct & Reputational Protection

  1. The Second Party shall maintain professionalism and avoid any act that could damage the First Party’s brand or public image.
  2. Any form of false, misleading, defamatory, or slanderous statements—whether written, verbal, or posted on social media—against the First Party shall result in:
    • Immediate termination of engagement.
    • Permanent blacklisting from future projects.
    • Legal action including but not limited to civil damages and defamation suits.
  3. The Second Party acknowledges that all concerns must be resolved internally through the grievance redressal process.

4. Grievance Redressal

  1. If dissatisfied with any service or project outcome, the Second Party must raise a formal written complaint within 48 hours of content delivery to: hello@vogueproductions.com.
  2. All grievances will be addressed through internal review and resolution mechanisms.

5. Compensation & Payment Terms

  1. Compensation shall be applicable only for paid/commercial projects. No payment shall be owed for collaborations or unpaid shoots.
  2. Payments shall be processed upon submission of a valid invoice by the Second Party.
  3. Payment disbursement shall occur within 15 business days of invoice receipt.

6. Ownership & Intellectual Property Rights

  1. All photographs, videos, and creative content produced through or under Vogue Productions shall remain the exclusive property of the First Party.
  2. The Second Party shall have no claim over copyright, distribution, or commercial use of the content.
  3. Use of content for personal portfolios is permitted, but modification or redistribution without written consent is strictly prohibited.

7. Portfolio & Career Opportunities

  1. A professional portfolio is essential to be considered for commercial projects.
  2. The First Party may support the Second Party through curated portfolio shoots, workshops, and industry networking.
  3. Project offers shall be based on merit, availability, and conduct.

8. Travel, Lodging & Styling

  1. For outstation assignments, the First Party shall cover travel, lodging, and meal costs unless otherwise agreed.
  2. Styling, makeup, and wardrobe for commercial shoots shall be arranged by the First Party.

9. Scheduling & Flexibility

  1. Shoot schedules will be shared in advance.
  2. The Second Party is not obliged to accept every shoot and may decline based on availability.
  3. Finalization of shoot dates shall be mutually agreed upon.

10. Confidentiality & Non-Disclosure

  1. The Second Party agrees not to disclose confidential details, trade secrets, or unreleased projects of the First Party.
  2. Breach of this clause will result in legal proceedings and blacklisting.

11. Termination & Blacklisting

  1. The First Party reserves the right to terminate this agreement without notice in case of breach, unprofessional behavior, or reputational harm.
  2. Grounds for permanent blacklisting include:
    • Failure to honor confirmed shoots without valid cause.
    • Defamation, slander, or false public statements.
    • Disclosure of confidential or proprietary information.

12. Legal Compliance & Governing Law

  1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.
